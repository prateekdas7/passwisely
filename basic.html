PassWisely

Statistics About Weak Passwords

  • Over 80% of data breaches are due to weak passwords.
  • Common passwords like "123456" and "password" are still widely used.
  • Using the same password across multiple sites increases risk.

Do's and Don'ts for Creating a Strong Password

Do's:

  • Use a mix of upper and lower case letters.
  • Include numbers and special characters.
  • Make it at least 12 characters long.
  • Use passphrases or a combination of words.

Don'ts:

  • Don't use easily guessable information (e.g., birthdays).
  • Don't use the same password for multiple accounts.
  • Don't use dictionary words or common phrases.
  • Don't share your password with anyone.
